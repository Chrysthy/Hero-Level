Hero Level Project

Available XP's

  • XP 1.000 = Iron
  • XP between 1.001 and 2.000 = Bronze
  • XP between 2.001 and 5.000 = Silver
  • XP between 6.001 and 7.000 = Gold
  • XP between 7.001 and 8.000 = Platinum
  • XP between 8.001 and 9.000 = Ascending
  • XP between 9.001 and 10.000= Immortal
  • XP greater than or equal 10.001 = Radiant
